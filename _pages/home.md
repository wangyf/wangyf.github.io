#### **Bio**
-----------------------
Yongfei Wang is a PhD candidate of the joint [<span style="color:default">*University of California, San Diego (Scripps/UCSD)*</span>](https://scripps.ucsd.edu)/[<span style="color:gray">*San Diego State University (SDSU)*</span>](https://www.sdsu.edu) Geophysics program. He, under supervision of Prof. **Steven Day** and Prof. **Peter Shearer**, has been working on observability of rupture dynamics and earthquake ground motion simulations. He will begin his postdoctoral research at [<span style="color:red">*Southern California Earthquake Center (SCEC)*</span>](https://www.scec.org), [<span style="color:red">*University of Southern California*</span>](https://www.usc.edu).

<br/>

#### **Research Area:**  
-----------------------
* Earthquake Rupture Dynamics
* Earthquake Strong Ground Motion
* High-performance Computation
* 3D Visualization
